City

The City is an Area of Swordmarch, accessible from the Great Sword's Hilt during Chapter 5. Later, it becomes a Region accessible from Farview Cape in Erythia Sea of the Cadensia Region. It is the home and base of operations of the Lost Numbers, the descendants of the first Ouroboros and the people they have freed from the Consuls' control.
